About Me

Engineer
with an Artist's Eye

I'm a final-year Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), navigating a world of signals, circuits, and frequencies.

Outside the lab, I'm a photographer drawn to the raw beauty of everyday life — street scenes, portraits, landscapes, and the golden hour light that transforms the ordinary into something worth remembering.

My camera is my way of slowing down and paying attention.
